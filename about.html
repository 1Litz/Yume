The Story Behind the World of Yume

Who We Are

Yume Private Server is a fan-made, custom-hosted Growtopia server created with love and vision by iLitz_. Our mission is to bring players a unique experience beyond the limits of the original game — one built with community, creativity, and fun.

Why We're Different

Yume isn't just another Growtopia clone. It’s a re-imagined server that values fairness, customization, and innovation. We’ve introduced tweaks and features not found in the original Growtopia, while still keeping that nostalgic sandbox charm.

Our Community

From casual builders to hardcore traders, our Discord is the heart of our community. Join us for events, giveaways, updates, and more!
