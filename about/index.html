Who We Are

Yume Private Server is where you can find new friends, meaningful interactions, and endless fun. We strive to bring players together in a joyful environment full of laughter and creativity. Daily giveaways keep the energy high, and our active development team ensures regular updates and improvements.

Why We're Different

Our economy is balanced, making it easy to farm, trade, and profit. We welcome suggestions from players and work hard to implement the best ideas. Regular themed events like Summertime Festivals, Chinese New Year, and Christmas parties ensure there's always something exciting happening on the server!

Build Your Dream World

Yume means “dream” in Japanese — and we mean it literally! Create the world of your dreams using creative tools, then share it with the community. Join our scheduled World Building Events to compete and win exclusive items as rewards!
