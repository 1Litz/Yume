Who We Are

Yume Private Server is a vibrant community where players can make new friends and enjoy meaningful interactions. We work hard to create joyful experiences filled with laughter and fun. This is the place you should be! Plus, we host giveaways almost every day for active members.

Why We're Different

Yume offers a balanced economy — perfect for farming, trading, and finding your own profitable path. We value player suggestions and strive to implement the best ideas from our community. With regular events, there's always something exciting happening on the server!

Build Your World With Your Own Creative Idea!

Yume gives you the tools to build the world of your dreams — quite literally! That’s why we chose the name "Yume", which means "Dream". Share your builds with us and join our scheduled World Building Events. Winners receive exclusive and unique items as rewards!
